FocusFuel Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

1. Data Collection

FocusFuel does not collect any personal data from users. All focus session data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with us.

Purchases are processed securely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information.

No third-party analytics or tracking is used in FocusFuel.

If you have questions about privacy, contact us at support@focusfuel.app.
